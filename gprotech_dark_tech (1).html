Core Technologies

GProTag & SmartFit — Innovation at the Edge

100% software-based, cloud-hosted solutions. No proprietary hardware — just smart mobile phones as registration and verification endpoints.

◈ SmartFit Engine

SmartFit — Live Facial Recognition

Guaranteed identification & verification using live facial images only. Automatically rejects photos, selfies, recorded videos, and 3D masks. Works through eyeglasses, beard, and partial face coverings.

✦ Fail-Proof Liveness · <0.3 sec verification
🏷️

GProTag — Object Securitization

Random image-print based unique tags affixed on certificates, documents, or assets. Stored in cloud data centers. Mobile app scan instantly validates genuineness and eliminates counterfeiting.

✦ Cloud secured · ✦ Instant mobile verification
☁️

Cloud-Native & Mobile First

Internet cloud hosted architecture with zero on-premise infrastructure. Smartphones act as registration and verification devices. Auto-scaling to PAN India service footprint with end-to-end encryption.

✦ Auto-scale · ✦ E2E encryption · ✦ 99.9% uptime
